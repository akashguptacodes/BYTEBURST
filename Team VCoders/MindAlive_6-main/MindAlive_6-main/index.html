About Us

Welcome to our sanctuary of mental health and wellness, where healing and growth intertwine to create a brighter tomorrow. At MindAlive , we believe in the transformative power of self-awareness, compassion, and community support.

We believe that taking care of your mental health is an ongoing practice. We believe in the value of a live experience with on-going support. We believe in community empowering each of us to give the strength, resilience and agency needed to live and feel our best.

At MindAlive, we are building the home for webinars, blogs, articles, newsletters and community. A place where connections and self discoveries are valued and accessible.

Frequently asked Questions

Stress can negatively impact mental health by contributing to the development or exacerbation of mental health disorders such as anxiety and depression, chronic stress can also weaken the body's immune system, increase the risk of physical health problems.

Genetics can play a significant role as certain genetic factors may predispose individuals to inherit. A susceptibility to certain mental health disorders. However genetics are just one factor and environmental influences also play a crucial role in the development of mental illness.

Peer support groups provide individuals with a safe and understanding environment to share experiences, receive validations and empathy, Gain coping strategies and reduce feelings of isolation.

Social media can both positives and negative effects on mental healths. It can provide social support and connections but may also contribute to the feelings of inadequacy loneliness and anxiety particularly when comparing oneself to others or experiencing cyber bullying.

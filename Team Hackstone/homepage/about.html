About us

Events

Technokratos is a fun event organized by the Computer Society of Software Engineers (CSSE). It offers different kinds of activities for everyone to enjoy. There are serious events like talking about technical stuff and solving problems together, and also fun things like singing, dancing, and acting. It's a great chance for people to show what they know and have a good time with others who like the same things.
